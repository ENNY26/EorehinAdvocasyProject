WHO WE ARE (OUR MISSION)

Hey there, we're the Jesus Foundation, glad you stopped by. Let's give you a quick rundown about us. We're all about tackling hunger worldwide.Imagine a world where nobody goes to bed hungry, where families enjoy three square meals a day without worry. Sounds nice, right? Unfortunately Lots of folks, including kids and babies, suffer from hunger every day and here at the Jesus Foundation, we're on a mission to change that.

Did You Know?

  • In 2022, 9.2% of the world's population, or 735 million people, faced chronic hunger.
  • 29.6% of the global population, or 2.4 billion people, were moderately or severely food insecure in 2022.
  • 828 million people went hungry every day in 2021, which is 150 million more than in 2019.

WHAT IS GOING ON TODAY IN TERMS OF HUNGER/POVERTY

At the Jesus Foundation, our core mission revolves around raising awareness about pressing global issues like hunger and poverty, shedding light on the intricate web of social injustice, power imbalances, and climate change contributing to these challenges. We believe that educating ourselves and others is not only a means of aiding those in need but also a profound reminder to cultivate gratitude for the blessings we possess. Our work extends beyond mere charity; it embodies a higher purpose, a calling to make a meaningful difference in the world
